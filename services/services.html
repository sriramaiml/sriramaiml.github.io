Our Services

Maintenance, Repairs and Annual Maintenance Contracts

Leemen Lifts offers comprehensive maintenance and repair services to ensure safe, reliable lift performance. With maintenance and repair on demand, as well as annual maintenance contracts (AMCs), our expert technicians provide routine checks, prompt repairs, and preventive care—keeping your lift operational, minimizing downtime, and extending its lifespan.

Maintenance

Leemen Lifts offers quick maintenance services whenever needed. Our technicians ensure your lift remains safe, operational, and well-maintained with just a call.

Repairs

In case of any issues, Leemen Lifts provides reliable, efficient repairs. We handle everything from minor adjustments to major fixes, restoring your lift promptly.

AMCs

Our AMCs include regular inspections and preventive care. With scheduled maintenance, you’ll enjoy a reliable, worry-free lift experience all year round.
